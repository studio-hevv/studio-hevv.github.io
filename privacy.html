Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Studio HEVV ("we," "us," or "our") builds apps designed to respect your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information in connection with our apps and website.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed to collect as little data as possible. Where data is collected, it is used solely to provide and improve the app's functionality.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Data Stored on Your Device

Some of our apps store data locally on your device. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless explicitly stated within the app.

Analytics

We may use Apple's built-in analytics features to understand aggregate app usage. This data is anonymous and does not identify individual users.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services (such as Apple's App Store infrastructure). These services have their own privacy policies that govern their data practices.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
